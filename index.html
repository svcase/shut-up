“You are the sky. Everything else is just the weather.” —Pema Chodron

Do this:

Take a moment to focus on each part of your body. Feel your arms at your side, your feet on the floor and your seat in the chair. Assess how you feel in each area. You can perform the body scan meditation lying down, seated or another posture. Notice if any part of your body is tense or tight. Focus on trying to soften or ease these areas and relax your muscles.
